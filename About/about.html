Ruby Rod

About

This is a choose-your-own-adventure game telling the Tale of Ruby Rod. You will follow the man of Benjamin Hornblower, navigating through him through many encounters and perils.
You will face pirates, krakens, cannibals and pirates.

You will be given choices throughout the game, these may heavily impact the story or have little to no consequence.
Nonetheless be wise with your choices.

To Save your progress in the game you will need to have an account, in order to begin where you left off.
You can Log In or Register in the Main Menu.
Alternatively you can still play without a an account, but cannot save your progress and must start at the beginning each time.
